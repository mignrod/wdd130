Home page

Our purpose is that our clients feel satisfied with our work, considering that they have been able to receive exceptional attention and service, and ensuring that they can feel safe in knowing and understanding the services they are seeking to access, the implication it has, and the possible solutions.

Benefits of our page
Clients, companies, and people, in general, are currently subject to the need to keep their mobile equipment and computers in the best possible condition to be able to carry out daily tasks and goals effectively. Due to this, preventive services become important to ensure the continuity of our operations better. Our customers got personal advice on how they can best maintain their devices.
This allows the data of those who access our website to be safe, beyond the personal information obtained once they confirm the service or purchase.
The section of information of interest is also available, where our clients can learn first-hand about the weekly promotions that are available or will be available soon, methods of direct contact with our servers, and the different plans that they can access on a daily basis.

How to contact us
Customers can contact us through our social networks. Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp, as well as through our telephone numbers or you can go to our offices during office hours.

Services

We provide a wide variety of services such as Computer and laptop repair, as well as preventive and corrective maintenance, installation of Operating Systems (OS), programming, Troubleshooting, and Configuration. Repair of cell phone (glass or screen changes, charge pin change, diagnosis, etc.), Unlocking, Flashing, Updating, preventive maintenance, configuration, and advice. Sale of cell phones of the best brands, accessories, chargers, tempered glass, headphones, and protectors. Personalized technical advice so that each client has the opportunity to know the product or service that are acquiring. Configuration and maintenance of Networks, servers, and Virtual Machines.

To access this information, customers simply have to access our website and explore the different options available, since the content is completely free. This is without underestimating that there may have personalized information that needs to be referred to the client verbally and professionally.

About Us

“JL Soluciones Tecnológicas” is a company with more than 20 years of experience in the area of specialized Technical Services for Cell Phones and Computers, which offers a wide range of services and solutions. We provide our clients with the advice and support they deserve when purchasing one of our products or services, as well as any technology-related products or services.

When purchasing a mobile device, most customers want to know about its performance and functionality, but cannot achieve this before purchasing it. Customers will be able to find detailed information on our products on our website; this makes it possible to have an effective comparison between their quality and functionality, as well as details of the services offered and the procedures in every case be followed. Accompanied by this information there are images and details that will allow them to see the operation or performance of the equipment, as well as a percentage relationship between the general opinion of the equipment, and the recommendation they receive from other users.
Free advice that allows them to find simple and quick solutions to the problems that their equipment is presenting. In addition, they can learn more about the methods used in the repair process and the technologies used, in order to have a better understanding of the processes to follow.

Why Us

What sets us apart from other services is our value commitment to constantly striving to provide exceptional service and products with quality and care superlatives. The most important thing for us is our clients and try to ensure that they can understand what situation they are facing, or how it is possible to solve it.

The experience acquired and the experiences of our clients make it possible for the process to continue improving at all times, refining details of the need and current realities adjusted to the most modern technological methods and procedures.

Constant training in the repair processes and methods available in the technological area makes our service maintain the highest standards, ensuring our clients an effective response to their needs.
